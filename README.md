# 📊 Customer Retention & Churn Analysis

## 🎯 Objective
Analyze customer and subscription data to identify churn patterns, understand customer behavior, and uncover key factors that influence customer retention. The goal is to generate actionable insights that help reduce churn and improve long-term customer engagement.

---

## 📁 Dataset
Telco Customer Churn Dataset (Kaggle)  
Includes customer demographics, services, contract types, payment methods, tenure, and churn status.

---

## 🔍 Analysis Areas
- Churn Rate Analysis  
- Customer Segmentation  
- Contract Type Impact  
- Customer Tenure Analysis  
- Monthly Charges vs Churn  
- Payment Method Analysis  

---

## 📈 Key Results
- Approximately 27% of customers have churned, indicating a moderate churn rate  
- Month-to-month contract customers show the highest churn  
- Customers tend to churn more during the early stages of their subscription  
- Higher monthly charges are associated with increased churn  
- Payment methods influence customer retention behavior  

---

## 💡 Recommendations
- Encourage customers to switch to long-term contracts through discounts and offers  
- Improve customer onboarding experience to reduce early churn  
- Introduce loyalty programs for long-term customers  
- Optimize pricing strategies to reduce churn among high-paying customers  
- Simplify and enhance payment processes for better user experience
